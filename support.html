This project would not exist without the generosity of many in our community.

Shipwreck City has been shaped by the kindness of individuals and organizations across the Seattle maritime community — people who opened their docks, lent their vessels, wrote letters on our behalf, and introduced us to the right people at the right time. This page is a record of that generosity.
